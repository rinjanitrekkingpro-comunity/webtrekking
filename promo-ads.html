2D1N Summit Trek Itinerary

Sembalun Route - Summit (3,726 m)

Day 1: Basecamp to Sembalun Crater Rim

07:00 AM transfer to Sembalun, register at park office. Hike through savanna grasslands to Pos 1, 2, and 3. Arrive at Sembalun Crater Rim (2,639m) around 16:30 for camping and sunset view.

Day 2: Summit Push & Descent to Sembalun

02:00 AM wake up call & light breakfast. Summit attack to 3,726m for sunrise. Return to camp for breakfast, then descend back to Sembalun Village (Finish around 14:00 PM).

3D2N Lake & Summit Itinerary

Sembalun Traverse to Senaru Route

Day 1: Sembalun to Crater Rim (Camp 1)

Hike through Sembalun savanna to Sembalun Crater Rim (2,639m). Overnight camp under the stars.

Day 2: Summit Push, Segara Anak Lake & Hot Springs (Camp 2)

02:30 AM summit push. After sunrise, descend to Segara Anak Lake (2,000m). Enjoy natural hot springs, hot lunch, and camp at Senaru Crater Rim or Lake area.

Day 3: Crater Rim to Senaru Rainforest (Finish)

Breakfast with crater view, descend through Senaru tropical rainforest. Arrive at Senaru Gate around 13:00 PM.

4D3N Complete Exploration

Relaxed Pace Full Rinjani Traverse

Day 1: Sembalun to Sembalun Rim

Relaxed hike from Sembalun to Crater Rim camp (2,639m).

Day 2: Summit Push & Segara Anak Lake Camp

Reach Summit 3,726m at sunrise. Descend slowly to Segara Anak Lake. Spend full afternoon relaxing & soaking in hot springs.

Day 3: Lake to Senaru Crater Rim Camp

Leisurely morning at the lake, trek up to Senaru Crater Rim (2,641m) for spectacular sunset views over Mount Agung Bali.

Day 4: Senaru Rim to Senaru Village

Descend through protected rainforest to Senaru Village (Finish).
