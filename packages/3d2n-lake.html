Package Overview

Designed for the true adventurer, this 3 Days 2 Nights trekking package is the ultimate way to experience the complete magic of Mount Rinjani. You will start your journey from Sembalun Village, trek through vast savannahs, and challenge yourself with the majestic summit push at 3,726 meters above sea level to witness a breathtaking sunrise over the Wallace Line.

After conquering the summit, descend to the magnificent Segara Anak Crater Lake. Rest your muscles in the healing natural hot springs, enjoy freshly cooked local meals prepared by our expert porters, and camp under a blanket of stars. We proudly operate with a 100% Zero-Waste policy, ensuring this beautiful ecosystem remains pristine for future generations.

Detailed Trekking Itinerary

Day 1: The Savannah Walk & Crater Rim

Sembalun Village (1,100m) ➔ Sembalun Crater Rim (2,639m) | ⏱️ Est. Trekking: 7-8 hours

  • Early morning pickup and transfer to Sembalun Village for National Park registration and health check.
  • Trek through beautiful open grasslands with clear, ascending views of the peak.
  • Enjoy a highly nutritious, freshly cooked hot lunch at POS 2 or POS 3 prepared by our team.
  • Conquer the steep ascent to Sembalun Crater Rim (Pelawangan Sembalun).
  • Settle into your premium tent, enjoy a spectacular sunset dinner, and sleep early to prepare for the summit.

Day 2: The Summit Push & Healing Lake

Crater Rim ➔ Summit (3,726m) ➔ Segara Anak Lake (2,000m) ➔ Senaru Rim (2,641m) | ⏱️ Est. Trekking: 10-11 hours

  • Wake up at 2:00 AM for a light pre-hike breakfast. Begin the challenging, steep summit push under a starry sky.
  • Reach the summit (3,726m) around 6:00 AM to witness an unforgettable sunrise spanning across Bali and Sumbawa.
  • Descend back to the campsite for a second, hearty breakfast to recover your energy.
  • Descend carefully to Segara Anak Lake. Relax and soothe your muscles in the natural volcanic hot springs.
  • After a fresh lakeside lunch, ascend to the Senaru Crater Rim for your second night of camping and another magical sunset.

Day 3: The Tropical Rainforest Descent

Senaru Crater Rim (2,641m) ➔ Senaru Village (601m) | ⏱️ Est. Trekking: 5-6 hours

  • Enjoy a final mountain breakfast with stunning morning views of Mount Agung (Bali) and the Gili Islands.
  • Begin the descent through the dense, shady tropical rainforest of Senaru, protecting you from the sun.
  • Spot rare black monkeys (Ebony Leaf Monkey) swinging through the canopy.
  • Our team will ensure all rubbish is packed and brought down (Zero-Waste commitment).
  • Arrive at Senaru Village around 1:00 PM for a final lunch before our private transport drops you off at your next destination.
