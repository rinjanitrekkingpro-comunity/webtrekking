Package Overview

This 3 Days 2 Nights trekking package is the most popular choice for adventurers looking to experience the complete magic of Mount Rinjani. You will start your journey from Sembalun Village, trek through vast savannahs, and challenge yourself with the majestic summit push at 3,726 meters above sea level to witness a breathtaking sunrise over the Wallace Line.

After conquering the summit, the journey continues down to the magnificent Segara Anak Crater Lake. Here, you can rest your muscles in the natural hot springs, camp by the lakeside under a blanket of stars, and finally descend through the lush tropical rainforests of Senaru.

Trekking Itinerary

Day 1: The Savannah Walk

Sembalun Village (1,100m) ➔ Sembalun Crater Rim (2,639m)

  • Early morning pickup from your hotel and transfer to Sembalun Village for registration.
  • Trek through beautiful open grasslands with clear views of the peak.
  • Lunch break at POS 2 or POS 3 prepared by our expert porters.
  • Steep ascent to Sembalun Crater Rim (Pelawangan Sembalun).
  • Enjoy the spectacular sunset, dinner, and overnight camp to prepare for the summit.

Day 2: The Summit Push & Lake Healing

Crater Rim (2,639m) ➔ Summit (3,726m) ➔ Segara Anak Lake (2,000m) ➔ Senaru Crater Rim (2,641m)

  • Wake up at 2:00 AM for a light breakfast and start the steep summit push under the stars.
  • Reach the summit (3,726m) around 6:00 AM to witness an unforgettable sunrise.
  • Descend back to the Sembalun campsite for a second, hearty breakfast.
  • Descend to Segara Anak Lake. Relax and swim in the healing natural hot springs.
  • After lunch, ascend to the Senaru Crater Rim for your second night of camping and another magical sunset.

Day 3: The Rainforest Descent

Senaru Crater Rim (2,641m) ➔ Senaru Village (601m)

  • Enjoy breakfast with a morning view of Mount Agung (Bali) and the Gili Islands.
  • Begin the descent through the dense, shady tropical rainforest of Senaru.
  • Spot rare black monkeys (Lutung) swinging through the canopy.
  • Arrive at Senaru Village around 1:00 PM for a final lunch.
  • Our transport will drop you off at your next destination in Lombok (Senggigi, Mataram, or Airport).
