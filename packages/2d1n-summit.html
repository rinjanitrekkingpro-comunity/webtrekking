Package Overview

Short on time but hungry for the ultimate challenge? The 2 Days 1 Night Summit package is tailored for hikers with a good fitness level who want to conquer the 3,726m peak of Mount Rinjani. You will ascend via the Sembalun route, known for its breathtaking rolling savannahs and direct path to the crater rim.

Experience a spectacular sunset from your campsite at the edge of the crater, followed by an adrenaline-pumping midnight push to the summit for an epic sunrise. Throughout this intense journey, our professional team will fuel you with freshly cooked, highly nutritious meals while strictly maintaining a 100% Zero-Waste trekking policy.

Detailed Trekking Itinerary

Day 1: The Savannah Ascent & Crater Rim

Sembalun Village (1,100m) ➔ Sembalun Crater Rim (2,639m) | ⏱️ Est. Trekking: 7-8 hours

  • Following an early pickup and breakfast, we head to Sembalun Village for the official National Park registration.
  • Begin the hike through the beautiful, exposed rolling savannahs (bring your sunblock!).
  • Stop at POS 2 or POS 3 where our porters will serve a delicious, freshly cooked hot lunch.
  • Tackle the famous "Regret Hills" (Bukit Penyesalan), a steep 3-hour ascent to the Sembalun Crater Rim.
  • Arrive at the campsite, enjoy a premium hot dinner overlooking Segara Anak Lake from above, and rest early.

Day 2: The Ultimate Summit Push & Descent

Crater Rim ➔ Summit (3,726m) ➔ Sembalun Village (1,100m) | ⏱️ Est. Trekking: 10-12 hours

  • Wake up at 2:00 AM for coffee and biscuits. Begin the 3-hour steep and sandy climb to the summit under the stars.
  • Reach the 3,726m summit just in time for an awe-inspiring sunrise illuminating the entire island of Lombok.
  • Descend back to the Crater Rim campsite. Enjoy a heavy, well-deserved second breakfast to recharge your muscles.
  • Pack up camp (ensuring zero waste is left behind) and begin the long descent back down the savannah to Sembalun Village.
  • Arrive at the basecamp by mid-afternoon where our transport awaits to drop you off at your next destination.
