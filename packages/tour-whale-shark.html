About This Adventure

Trade the high altitudes of Mount Rinjani for the deep blue waters of Sumbawa. Saleh Bay is one of the few places in the world where you can sustainably swim alongside magnificent, wild Whale Sharks in their natural habitat.

This is a truly magical, once-in-a-lifetime marine encounter. Our expert local guides ensure a respectful and eco-friendly interaction with these gentle giants as they surface to feed at dawn.

Typical Itinerary

11:00 PM (Previous Night)

Lombok Departure

Late-night pick up from your hotel in Lombok. We will drive to Kayangan Port and take the midnight ferry crossing to Sumbawa Island.

04:00 AM

Sail to Saleh Bay

Arrive at the harbor in Sumbawa and board our private charter boat. Enjoy the calm night breeze as we sail towards the whale shark point.

06:00 AM

Sunrise & Whale Shark Encounter

As the sun rises, jump into the water! Swim respectfully alongside the majestic whale sharks as they feed around the local fishing platforms (Bagan).

08:30 AM

Breakfast on Boat

Warm up with hot coffee or tea and a delicious local breakfast served freshly on the boat while cruising back to the mainland.

03:00 PM

Return to Lombok

Arrive safely back at your hotel in Lombok, bringing home unforgettable memories of the gentle giants.
