Tetebatu Walking Tour

Rice Terraces & Monkey Forest

Overview

Experience the peaceful countryside of Lombok! Tetebatu is famous for its stunning, endlessly green rice terraces that rival those in Bali. Walk through local farms, spot the endemic Black Monkeys (Lutung) in the monkey forest, and taste authentic local coffee.

Tour Highlights

  • Guided walk through panoramic Rice Terraces.
  • Visit the Monkey Forest (home to the Black Macaques).
  • See the beautiful Sarang Walet Waterfall.
  • Learn how locals process coffee, vanilla, and spices.
