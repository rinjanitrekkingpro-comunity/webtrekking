Bukit Pergasingan

The Best Sunrise Spot over Sembalun Valley

Overview

Not ready for Mount Rinjani? Bukit Pergasingan (Pergasingan Hill) is the ultimate alternative. Standing at 1,700 meters above sea level, this 2-3 hour soft hike rewards you with a spectacular sunrise and an iconic view of the colorful, patchwork-like farming grid of Sembalun village with Mount Rinjani towering in the background.

Sunrise Itinerary

  • 03:00 AM: Pick up from Sembalun area & start hiking.
  • 05:30 AM: Arrive at the summit of Pergasingan Hill just in time for sunrise.
  • 06:00 AM: Enjoy a light breakfast & coffee overlooking the valley.
  • 08:00 AM: Start descending back to Sembalun village.
  • 10:00 AM: Finish tour.
