We take pride in being a fully Licensed & Trusted Operator, completely registered under the Mount Rinjani National Park Authority (TNGR).

We prioritize high-energy, nutritious meals prepared fresh at camp to keep your stamina optimal throughout the strenuous trek.

Your Safety is Non-Negotiable

Climbing to a height of 3,726 meters leaves no room for operational compromises or cutting corners. When you choose a licensed team, you ensure that if an emergency arises—whether a severe sprain or sudden altitude sickness—there is an immediate, coordinated system ready to transport you down safely.
