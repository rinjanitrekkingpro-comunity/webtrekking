The Healing Magic of Segara Anak Hot Springs

After a long day of trekking, nothing beats the feeling of soaking in natural volcanic hot springs. Segara Anak, the turquoise crescent lake of Mount Rinjani, is home to these mystical waters.

A Sacred Place

For the locals, Segara Anak is a sacred place. The hot springs near the lake are believed to have incredible healing properties for both the body and the soul. The water is rich in sulfur and minerals, perfect for relaxing your sore muscles after the summit push.

What to Expect?

  • Natural Pools: There are several natural pools with different temperatures.
  • Stunning Views: Soak while looking at the majestic "Barujari" volcano inside the crater.
  • Local Experience: Learn about the local Sasak myths surrounding the lake from our expert guides.
