Dirty Clothes After the Trek? We Have the Solution

25 February 2026 | Admin Rinjani Pro

You did it! You conquered the summit of Mount Rinjani, soaked in the hot springs, and witnessed the magical Segara Anak lake. You return to Senaru or Sembalun basecamp feeling exhausted but incredibly proud.

However, when you open your backpack, reality hits: a pile of dirty, sweaty, and volcanic dust-covered clothes. If you are catching a flight to Bali or heading straight to the Gili Islands the next day, packing foul-smelling gear into your pristine luggage is the last thing you want to do.

Introducing Our Exclusive Trekker's Laundry Service

At Rinjani Trekking Pro, we understand that true hospitality doesn't end when you step off the mountain. That is why we offer an exclusive, express laundry service specifically designed for our trekkers.

No need to hassle with dirty laundry on your flight or search for local laundromats in a rush. We handle the mess so you don't have to.

How Our Service Works:

  • Drop Off: Once you finish your trek and arrive at our basecamp, simply hand over your dirty trekking clothes to our team.
  • Express Cleaning: We use high-quality, fabric-safe detergents to remove stubborn volcanic ash and sweat.
  • Fresh and Ready: Your clothes will be washed, dried, and neatly folded, ready to be packed in your luggage before your next departure or transfer.

Travel Light and Stress-Free

By utilizing our laundry service, you avoid the risk of moldy clothes in your suitcase and save yourself from extra baggage fees caused by heavy, wet gear. You can continue your Indonesian holiday feeling fresh and clean.
