When is the Best Time to Climb Mount Rinjani in 2026?

Planning to conquer the second-highest volcano in Indonesia? Timing is everything. To witness the legendary sunrise at 3,726m, you need to know the weather patterns of Lombok island.

The Trekking Season (April - December)

Mount Rinjani National Park officially opens from **April 1st to December 31st**. Outside these months, the park is usually closed for the rainy season and ecosystem recovery.

Peak Season (July - August)

This is the driest time of the year. Expect clear blue skies and perfect visibility. However, these months are very busy, so we recommend booking your permit at least 2 months in advance.

Weather Guide by Month

  • April - May: The landscape is incredibly green and lush. Occasional rain showers, but very fresh air.
  • June - September: The best weather. Dry, sunny, but the nights at the crater rim can be very cold (down to 5°C).
  • October - December: Transition to rainy season. Warmer temperatures but higher chance of mist and clouds.
