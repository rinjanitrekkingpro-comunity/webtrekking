Senaru Waterfalls & Village Tour

Discover Sendang Gile & Tiu Kelep

Overview

A perfect half-day escape! Walk through the lush tropical rainforest of Senaru to witness two of Lombok's most famous waterfalls: Sendang Gile and the magical Tiu Kelep. Afterward, immerse yourself in local culture by visiting the authentic Senaru Traditional Village.

Tour Itinerary

  • 09:00 AM: Pick up from your hotel in Senaru.
  • 09:30 AM: Start walking down to the first waterfall, Sendang Gile.
  • 10:30 AM: River crossing and jungle trek to the majestic Tiu Kelep Waterfall (swimming allowed).
  • 01:00 PM: Lunch at a local restaurant.
  • 02:30 PM: Visit the Senaru Traditional Sasak Village to see ancient local houses.
  • 04:00 PM: Return to hotel.
