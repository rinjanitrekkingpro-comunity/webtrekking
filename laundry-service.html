Trek Light, Stay Fresh!

Exclusive Laundry Service for Hikers

Why carry dirty clothes to the mountain?

We understand that many backpackers and tourists often arrive at Senaru with dirty clothes from previous travels. Instead of carrying that extra, smelly weight up Mount Rinjani, leave it with us!

1. Drop Off

Leave your dirty laundry at our basecamp before you start your trek.

2. We Wash & Iron

We professionally wash, dry, and neatly fold your clothes while you hike.

3. Pick Up

Collect your fresh, clean-smelling clothes right after you descend!

  • Express Service (Same Day) IDR 40,000 / Kg
